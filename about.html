Meet the Designer

I am a designer currently studying Interactive Arts and Technology at Simon Fraser University to achieve my Bachelor of Arts. I have an interest in user interface and experience design, and making unique and intuitive experiences. I believe that collaboration, creativity and adequate research are the key ingredients needed to create successful, user-friendly applications. Through my schooling and other opportunities I have exercised my visual design, user-research, user-testing and programming skills.

Being a student I am constantly learning and seeking new opportunities to grow my experiences and skillset. I hope to pursue a career in user interface and user experience design and utilize my skills and passions in front-end design. In my future career I want to work in a company that allows me to develop and grow my skills and seize new opportunities.
